Our Mission

The Open Church is creating the first church in Oklahoma that stays open 24/7, 365 days a year, providing food, clothing, and shelter to all who come seeking help. We believe in radical hospitality and unconditional love for every person, regardless of their circumstances.

"People will not be turned away because of drugs & alcohol. Instead, we provide on-site drug and alcohol counselors, mental health professionals, priests, and monks who dedicate their lives to serving those in need."

Our vision is to create a sanctuary where everyone is welcome, where basic human needs are met, and where people can find hope, healing, and purpose through community, prayer, and practical skill-building. We're building more than just a church - we're building a community of transformation.

What We Offer

24/7 Shelter & Housing

Open every day of the year with 200 campsites, dormitory-style housing, and emergency shelter. Showers, bathrooms, laundry facilities, and basic amenities available to all.

Community Kitchen & Dining

Three nutritious meals served daily in our community dining hall. The kitchen is open 24/7 for snacks and beverages. We also provide food pantry services for those in need.

24-Hour Prayer & Praise Room

A dedicated sacred space open around the clock for prayer, meditation, worship, and reflection. All faith traditions are welcome to use this peaceful sanctuary.

Tech Skills Training School

Teaching practical skills like app development, video game design, website creation, radio station operation, and more. Our goal is to provide marketable skills for sustainable employment.

Counseling & Support Services

Onsite drug and alcohol counselors, mental health professionals, life coaches, and spiritual directors available to support residents on their journey to wholeness.

Clothing & Basic Necessities

Free clothing closet, personal care items, blankets, and other essentials available to all who come. Donations from the community help stock our resource center.
